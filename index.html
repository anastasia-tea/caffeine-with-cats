About Us

Here at Caffeine with Cats, we pride ourselves on creating a safe, happy environment for local stray cats.

After opening in 20XX, we have helped home over 200 cats that were seeking homes and connected them with loving families in and around the area.

Treasuring the café experience and kindly-crafted coffee and tea, we opened up a café inside our cats' home.

Understanding adopting a cat is not always possible for cat-lovers at any given time, we wanted to provide a location allowing people to spend time with their favorite friends without an expectation of adoption as well as those who are looking to add a cat friend to their home.

We work hard to curate a healthy, peaceful environment for our cats and look forward to seeing you here!
